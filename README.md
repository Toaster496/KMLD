# KMLD Plant Selector

A private plant library for your landscape design business. Share plants with your students and help them build their own plant palettes for projects.

---

## What This App Does

This is a simple tool where you can:
- **Share plants** with your students in a curated library
- **Students can browse** all plants by category (Trees, Shrubs, Ground Covers, Perennials)
- **Students can save** their favorite plants to create a plant palette
- **Export palettes** as PDF or copy to clipboard for their projects

---

## How Students Access It

1. You generate an invite link from the Admin Dashboard
2. Send the link to your student
3. They enter their name and can start using it immediately
4. Their favorites are saved automatically

---

## How to Use It

### As an Admin (You)

**Getting Started**
- Open the app and log in with your admin account
- Click "Admin Mode" in the top right corner

**Managing Students**
- Click "Generate Invite Link" to create a new access link
- Copy the link and send it to your student
- See all registered students in the "Manage Students" panel
- Rename or remove students if needed

**Managing Plants**
- Click "Add Plant" to add a new plant to your library
- Click the pencil icon on any plant to edit it
- Click the trash icon to delete a plant

**Quick Stats**
- See total number of plants in your library
- See how many active students you have

### As a Student

**Browsing Plants**
- Click a category button (Trees, Shrubs, Ground Covers, Perennials) to filter
- Click sub-category buttons for more specific filtering
- Use the search bar to find plants by name
- Click "Perennials" and you can also filter by flower color

**Viewing Plants**
- Click any plant card to see full details including dimensions and notes

**Saving Favorites**
- Click the heart icon on any plant to save it to your favorites
- Switch to the "Favourites" tab to see all your saved plants

**Exporting Your Palette**
- Click "Copy List" to copy all your favorites to clipboard (paste into Word, etc.)
- Click "Download PDF" to get a professional-looking document with images

## Plant Categories

| Category | Sub-Categories |
|----------|---------------|
| **Trees** | Deciduous, Evergreen, Screening, Feature |
| **Shrubs** | Flowering, Foliage, Hedging, Native |
| **Ground Covers** | Strappy Leaf, Mass Planting, Spreading, Native |
| **Perennials** | Cut Flower, Aromatic, Border, Native |
| **Trees & Shrubs** | Deciduous, Evergreen |

For Perennials, you can also filter by color: Red, Orange, Yellow, Pink, Purple, Blue, White.

## Quick Start

1. **Add your plants** - Click "Add Plant" and fill in the details
2. **Generate invite links** for your students
3. **Students browse and favorite** plants for their projects
4. **Students download** their plant palette as a PDF
